About MCA

The Department of MCA focuses on bridging the gap between industry and academia. Eminent industry professionals are invited to guide students and faculty members.

"Welcome to the MCA program! We are dedicated to providing a robust foundation in computer applications, focusing on emerging technologies like AI, Data Analytics, and Mobile Development. Our experienced faculty, industry-aligned curriculum, and hands-on projects ensure you gain the skills to excel in the dynamic tech world. We look forward to guiding you towards innovation and a successful IT career."
